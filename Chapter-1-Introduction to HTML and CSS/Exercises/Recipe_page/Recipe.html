Fried Chicken Recipe

Description:

Fried chicken, also known as Southern fried chicken, is a crispy, seasoned chicken dish that retains its meat's juices and can be pan-fried, deep-fried, pressure-fried, or air-fried.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (4 pound) chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour for coating
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 quarts vegetable oil for frying

instruction:

  1. Cut chicken into pieces and skin them.
  2. Place flour in a plastic bag, season with paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Dip chicken pieces in buttermilk, then coat in flour.
  4. Place coated chicken on a baking sheet or tray.
  5. Cover with a clean dish towel or waxed paper.
  6. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet.
  7. Brown chicken in hot oil on both sides.
  8. Cook for 30 minutes, then reduce heat and continue frying until crispy.
  9. Drain fried chicken on paper towels.
  10. Keep finished chicken in a warm oven while preparing the rest.
