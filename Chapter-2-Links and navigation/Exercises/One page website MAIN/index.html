Overview

Dipper and Mabel Pines, twins, are taken to the mysterious town of Gravity Falls, Ore, to spend the summer with their great-uncle, Grunkle Stan. Grunkle Stan owns a tourist trap called The Mystery Shack, which he runs with the kids' assistance. The twins make an effort to fit in, but they soon realize that Gravity Falls is different, and they set out to discover its mysteries. Dipper and Mabel utilize their fervent desire to defeat evil and a mysterious notebook they find that provides clues to the mysteries of the town to battle the approaching mysteries that threaten to envelop them.

Plot

The show centers on the adventures of Dipper Pines (Jason Ritter) and his twin sister Mabel (Kristen Schaal), who are assigned to spend the summer in Gravity Falls, Oregon, a mysterious town known for paranormal happenings and otherworldly creatures, with their great-uncle Stan (also known as "Grunkle") Hirsch.

Reviews

Both reviewers and viewers have given Gravity Falls high praise. It is acclaimed for its deft writing, compelling puzzles, and fully realized characters.
